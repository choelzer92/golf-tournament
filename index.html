Hog Suckers vs Junkyard Dawgs | 136 Total Points

Day 1 - Old Trail | Thu June 11

2v2 Combined Stableford (38 pts)

  • Two matches, each pair combines their stableford points per hole
  • Higher combined team wins the hole (1 pt), tie = 0.5 each
  • 18 holes = 18 pts per match (36 total)
  • Best individual stableford round = 2 pts to that player's team
  • Full handicap (100%)

Stableford Points (net score):

Triple+: -1 Double: 0 Bogey: 1 Par: 2 Birdie: 3 Eagle: 4 Albatross+: 5

Day 2 - Spring Creek | Fri June 12

Best Net + Best Gross per Hole (38 pts)

  • Each team plays as a foursome, all play own ball
  • Per hole: take the best NET score (1 player) and best GROSS score (a different player)
  • Sum both scores vs par, compare teams per nine
  • Front 9 winner: 16 pts | Back 9 winner: 16 pts | Overall: 4 pts
  • Junk (2 pts): gross birdies (1), eagles (2), team pars (1)
  • CTP front and back are side bets only
  • 80% handicap

Day 3 - Glenmore | Sat June 13

2v2 Best Ball + 1v1 Match Play (60 pts)

Front 9: 2v2 Best Ball Match Play

  • 2 matches, best ball per team per hole
  • Win hole = 1 pt, halve = 0.5 each
  • Match winner gets +1 bonus point
  • Total front: ~20 pts distributed
  • 90% handicap, strokes from lowest player

Back 9: 1v1 Match Play

  • 4 individual matches
  • Win hole = 1 pt, halve = 0.5 each
  • Match winner gets +1 bonus point
  • Total back: ~40 pts distributed
  • 100% of handicap difference, strokes on hardest holes
  • Back 9 strokes = floor(diff/2) — odd holes go to front
